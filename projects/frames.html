Les Salins des Pesquiers is a former saltworks site located on the Giens Peninsula in Hyères, southern France. Nestled between the Mediterranean Sea and saltwater lagoons, this unique natural area offers stunning landscapes shaped by centuries of salt production. Today, the site is a protected ecological haven, home to a rich biodiversity, including flamingos and other migratory birds.

Cadres is an artist residency project designed for photographers practicing film photography. This project highlights the unique framing approach characteristic of these artists, focusing on the creation of small observatories, or “grafts.” These structures, added to the roof, form frames that showcase the nature and landscapes of the salt flats.
